About Ntsetse Flyers FC

More Than a Club — One Family

Mission

Ntsetse Flyers Football Club exists to provide an inclusive, disciplined, and supportive environment where everyone is welcomed as family. We develop football talent, life skills, and strong values while proudly representing our community with passion, integrity, and unity.

Our Story

To be a united and respected football family that nurtures talent, builds character, and transforms lives on and off the field

Values

  • Family & Unity – We are more than a club; we are one family where everyone belongs.
  • Inclusivity – Football is for everyone, and no one is left behind.
  • Discipline & Respect – We uphold high standards of conduct on and off the pitch.
  • Development – We grow players holistically, both as athletes and as people.
  • Integrity – We act honestly, humbly, and responsibly at all times.
  • Commitment – We work hard, stay dedicated, and give our best for the badge.
  • Community – We uplift and serve our community through football

Who We Are

Ntsetse Flyers Football Club is a community-based, family-oriented football club built on unity, respect, and opportunity. We are more than just a football team — we are a home where everyone is welcomed, supported, and valued as part of one united family.

Our Story

Ntsetse Flyers was founded with a simple but powerful purpose: to create a positive and inclusive environment where football is used as a tool for growth, discipline, and transformation. The club was established to give individuals from all walks of life a place to belong, develop, and thrive both on and off the pitch.

Our Club Culture

Our culture is built around togetherness, accountability, and mutual respect. At Ntsetse Flyers, everyone matters, and success is measured not only by results but by character and unity.

  • Family-first environment
  • Respect and discipline on and off the pitch
  • Strong sense of unity and belonging
  • Support beyond football
  • Pride in representing the community

Our Football Philosophy

We believe football should be played with passion, discipline, and teamwork. Our approach focuses on continuous improvement, commitment, and respect for the game. While competition is important, personal development and collective growth always come first.

Inclusivity & Accommodation

Ntsetse Flyers is a club that accommodates everyone. Regardless of background, age, or ability, we strive to provide a safe, welcoming, and supportive environment where all members feel accepted and empowered. Football is for all, and so is our family.

Community Involvement

The club is deeply committed to uplifting the community through positive role modeling, mentorship, and youth engagement. We aim to inspire unity, responsibility, and hope through our actions on and off the field.

Our Standards

All members of Ntsetse Flyers are expected to uphold the highest standards of discipline, respect, teamwork, and integrity. These principles guide everything we do as a club and as a family.
